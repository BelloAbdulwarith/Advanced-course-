Course Overview

This advanced course is designed to provide in-depth mastery of Arabic and Islamic sciences. Delve into Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, and advanced Arabic linguistics, preparing you for higher studies and intellectual engagement.

Course Objectives

  • Achieve proficiency in advanced Arabic grammar, rhetoric, and linguistics.
  • Deepen understanding of Qur’anic sciences and Tafseer methodologies.
  • Gain expertise in analyzing and authenticating Hadith literature.
  • Build a solid foundation in Islamic jurisprudence and contemporary applications.
  • Enhance critical thinking and analytical skills for classical Islamic texts.

Key Features

Master grammar, syntax, and rhetoric, with a focus on classical text analysis and advanced translation skills.

Explore complex Qur'anic themes, revelation contexts, and linguistic miracles through advanced Tafseer methods.

Analyze the major Hadith collections, study methods of authentication, and understand their application in Islamic rulings.

Learn comparative Fiqh and discuss modern applications, focusing on ethics, family law, and societal issues.

Understand the contributions of Islamic scholars, key historical events, and the legacy of Islamic civilization.

Learning Outcomes

  • Mastery of advanced Arabic language skills, including grammar, rhetoric, and composition.
  • Ability to interpret and analyze Qur'anic and Hadith texts with critical insight.
  • Comprehensive understanding of Fiqh and its modern applications.
  • Deep connection with Islamic theology, history, and cultural contributions.
  • Preparedness for university-level studies in Arabic and Islamic sciences.

Course Duration

The course spans 48 weeks, with 4 sessions per week, each lasting an hour. Flexible online and in-person learning options are available to accommodate all students.

Who Is This Course For?

This course is ideal for advanced students with foundational knowledge in Arabic and Islamic studies, preparing them for university-level programs or personal mastery.
